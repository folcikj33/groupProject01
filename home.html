City Details

The "city details" panel below will pull a top level summary about the city used in the job search.

The city data is pulled from Teleport. Teleport is a company that provides data to help users make an informed decision about the quality of life in the cities relevant to their job search. Teleport's data spans across 200 international cities.

Below includes some insight into the catagories provided by Teleport:

Housing: a score derived from rental costs of small to large apartments.

Cost of Living: a score derived from average cost of goods from cab rides, groceries, lunch, gym memberships, and more.

Startups: a score derived from average monthly increase of startups, number of investors, meetup events, and more.

Venture Capital: a score derived from local venture capital opportunities.

Travel Connectivity: a score derived from public transport and airport traffic.

Commute: a score derived from intercity traffic.

Business Freedom: a score derived from labor restrictions, time it takes to open a business, and more.

Safety: a score derived from crime rate, gun related deaths and more.

Healthcare: a score derived from life expectancy, healthcare costs and more.

Education: a score derived from high school student testing performance, nearby university rankings, and more.

Environmental Quality: a score derived from air quality, parks, drinking water quality and more.

Economy: a score derived GDP per capita and GDP growth rate.

Taxation: a score derived from sales tax, income tax, and more.

Internet Access: a score derived from internet speeds.

Leisure & Culture: a score derived from nearby art galleries, cinemas, concert venues, and more.

Tolerance: a score derived from LGBT rights and tolerance towards minorities.

Outdoors: a score derived from hill/mountain presence, urban area elevation, water access, and more.
